Hot Dogs

Description

These hot dogs remind me of lazy Saturday afternoons, watching the game.

Once you taste these tasty hot dogs, you will never be the same.

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Sausages
  • Cheese
  • Minced beef
  • Chilli powder

Steps

  1. Two tablespoons of cinnamon
  2. And two or three egg whites
  3. Half a stick of butter
  4. Mmmmmmmmelted
  5. Stick it all in a bowl baby
  6. Stir it with a wooden spoon
  7. Half a cup of flour
  8. You'll be in heaven soon
