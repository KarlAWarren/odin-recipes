Burger Sauce

Description

This burger sauce will add zing to any barbecue.

McDonalds, SchmcDonalds... this burger sauce takes the Big Mac Sauce's pocket money and sends it home, crying to mummy.

Ingredients

  • Buffalo sauce
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Salt
  • Pickled onions, diced

Steps

  1. Literally, throw it all into a small bowl
  2. Mix it together
  3. Spread it on your toasted buns
  4. If you can't do this... just give up
