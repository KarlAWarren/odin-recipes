Lasagna

Description

This lasagna resipe has been passed down through the generations of my family. My dear sweet grandma used to make this for us every Sunday evening.

Once you taste this delicious lasagna, you'd be forgiven for thinking that you've been possessed by the spirit of Garfield.

Ingredients

  • Minced beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Flour
  • Pasta sheets
  • Olive oil
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Throw everything into the pan
  2. Turn on the heat
  3. Mix it all up together with a spoon
  4. Probably burn it, like my nan did
  5. Order pizza instead
